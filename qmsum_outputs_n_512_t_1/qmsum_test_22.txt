[B] 5,000 euros
[C] 10,000 euros
[D] 12,000 euros

[B] 5,000 euros

Based on the project manager's statement, 5,000 euros were allocated in the project budget, and 1 euro was left over. Therefore, the correct answer is (B) 5,000 euros.
