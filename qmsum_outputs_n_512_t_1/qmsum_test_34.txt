[ANSWER]
The Public Policy Institute for Wales (PPIW) evaluated this proposed policy and concluded that it would have no substantial impacts on net income, poverty or work behaviour for families with children, and that the impact on work participation and work hours for mothers and families with a child of target age was extremely small.
