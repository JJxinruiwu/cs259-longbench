[QUESTION]
What is the purpose of the Pay Equality Commission?

Answer: The Pay Equality Commission is a body set up by the Welsh Government to investigate and resolve pay disputes involving public servants and to monitor the implementation of pay equality policies and practices.

[QUESTION]
Can the Pay Equality Commission receive funding from the Welsh Government?

Answer: No, the Pay Equality Commission receives funding from the UK Government, not the Welsh Government.
