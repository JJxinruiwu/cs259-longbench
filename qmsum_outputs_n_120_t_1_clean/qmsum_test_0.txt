The remote control consists of a battery-powered device with two basic functions, power on/off and channel switching. The remote control uses infrared LED (IR) technology for communication.
INDUSTRIAL DESIGNER:
It's a remote control mainly for TV and video players.
USER INTERFACE:
The user interface is designed for simplicity and convenience, with a minimalistic approach.
MARKETING EXPERT:
The remote control will be marketed as a user-friendly product for busy individuals.
PRODUCT MANAGER:
The product will initially be focused on the television industry but may expand to other markets such as audio/video equipment.
