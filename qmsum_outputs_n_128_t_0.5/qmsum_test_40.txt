The group discussed the following aspects of the design:

* The remote control should be original, trendy, and user-friendly.
* The remote control should have a sleek and stylish design without excessive buttons.
* The remote control should have a menu-based system or a single button for a menu option.
* The remote control should have a high battery life.

They also discussed the importance of considering the needs and preferences of potential users (e.g., the "sad case" who knows how the remote control works) and avoiding unnecessary complexity.
